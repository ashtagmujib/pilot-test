What is Charitok?

Charitok ($TOK) is the world’s first meme coin that turns every transaction into real-world help for underprivileged communities. From food and water to education and rent, we use crypto chaos to create real change. we are on a mission to trun the potential profits from the chaos of the crypto space into something that benefits underdeveloped and underprivileged people and communities

How It Works

Every transaction supports real aid via our charity wallet. Here's how it's split:

  • 4% to public Charity Wallet
  • 3% to Liquidity Pool
  • 2% reflections to holders
  • 1% dev + operations (capped & transparent)

Charity funds will be fully tracked on-chain. All donations are documented with receipts and proof-of-help.
