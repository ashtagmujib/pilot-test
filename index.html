What is Charitok?

Charitok ($TOK) is the world’s first meme coin that turns every transaction into real-world help for underprivileged communities in Nigeria and beyond. From food and water to education and rent — we use crypto chaos to create real change.

How It Works

Every transaction supports real aid via our charity wallet. Here's how it's split:

  • 4% to public Charity Wallet
  • 3% to Liquidity Pool
  • 2% reflections to holders
  • 1% dev + operations (capped & transparent)

Charity funds are used based on community votes and are fully tracked on-chain. All donations are documented with receipts and proof-of-help.
